About Me

My name is Shantella, and I'm a licensed Cosmetologist with 10 years of experience in the beauty industry. I enjoy creating and transforming fun and exciting looks with my clients.

As a married mom of 5, my life is full of joy and adventure. Balancing my career and family has taught me valuable lessons in time management, patience, and creativity. My children are my biggest inspiration, and their support motivates me to pursue new challenges.

Recently, I've embarked on an exciting journey to learn programming. I've always been fascinated by technology, and now I'm exploring the world of web development and programming languages. It's a new chapter in my life, and I'm thrilled to combine my creative skills with problem-solving through coding.

Join me on this incredible journey as I share my experiences, insights, and discoveries in the world of programming. Stay tuned for more posts and updates!
