I will Cut You

Today was rough and that's putting it lightly. So bare with me, I need to vent a little about something that has been cropping up more often than split ends in the salon: clients who don't book appointments appropriately. Just when I get ready to enjoy my lunch a client appears out of thin air, requesting "just a quick little hair cut and a blowout. Maybe we can voer my grays too". Now, don't get me wrong, I understand that life can get hectic, and hair emergencies do happen. But sometimes it feels like I'm being stretched thinner than a strand of hair during a high ponytail. My schedule is meticulously organized, with clients' appointments carefully slotted to ensure everyone receives my love and attention. So, when clients disregard the art of planning and expect me to be an instant hair magician, it's like they're playing a game of "hair appointment roulette." And trust me, it's not the kind of gamble I enjoy. I can't just whisk them into my chair and make their hair dreams come true without compromising the quality and care I strive to provide. It's essential to understand that hairstyling is an art that requires time, precision, and the occasional sprinkle of magic hair dust. I gonna need yall to get your lives and book accordingly! Otherwise yall gonna be out here looking cray cray.

Remember, I'm here to create stunning transformations and make you feel like the fabulous individuals you are. But even the most talented hairstylists need a bit of breathing room to craft those perfect layers, bouncy curls, and sleek updos. I won't physically "cut" you (I promise!), but I do kindly request that you respect the artist by booking appointments in a timely manner. Together, we can maintain your luscious locks and keep my sanity intact. Let's conquer the world of fabulous hair, one well-planned appointment at a time! Okay, rant over! Now, back to making magic happen, one snip at a time. Stay fabulous and keep rocking those gorgeous hairstyles!
